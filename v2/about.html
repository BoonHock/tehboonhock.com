About me

I am currently pursuing B. Eng. (Hons) Mechanical Engineering in Taylor's University. While studying, I also learn web development as an interest. My skills include HTML5, CSS3 (+ Bootstrap), JavaScript (+ jQuery), PHP and SQL (MySQL).

During my studies, I was involved in various projects such as enhancement of graphene oxide nanofluid, design and develop shoes for the elderly, develop a control system to save electricity, etc. My involvement in these projects has been profound. I learnt to be a better team player to enhance my team's dynamics, presentation skills and many more besides gaining a lot of technical knowledge such as Arduino programming, electrical circuits, design process and observation techniques.

My professional experience include an internship with New Hoong Fatt, a car parts manufacturer, where I worked on three challenging projects. My main project was to identify root cause of dent detects in metal parts produced. Go to Projects page for more information. The knowledge and experience that I gained from this internship were priceless.

Furthermore, my initiative personality pushed me to apply for Scholars@Work Mentoring Programme during my first year of studies in Taylor's. This programme kickstarted my programming "career". I was required to build an Excel VBA program for School of Engineering to manage student transcript database.

I like to explore and learn. Thus, I created two websites, namely tehboonhock.com and airbald.com. The sole purpose of these websites were to learn. Not too much thoughts were put into the aesthetics of the websites so they may look a little odd. After gaining a sound knowledge off building websites that include server side programming, I decided to improve my web design skills a little bit. Hence, tehboonhock.com - Version 2.
