      The Weird Monk

      I was a one of the good students in class when I was in Form 1 to 3 but it all changed when I entered Form 4. I started failing my subjects and all I cared about was to play basketball. I skipped classes with my friends and play basketball all they long. My school's discipline was bad so I had no issue skipping classes yet playing basketball in school during school time. But around July 2011, I woke up. I met a monk and I do not why but I started talking to him. I told him I want to achieve great things in life but studying is just not something that I enjoy doing. He then gave me a card.

      He then told me something like,

      "You are like the Monkey King. Powerful yet foolish. You can do great things but you chose to do stupid things. You are unbeatable but you beat yourself."

      (translated) The Weird Monk

      I started studying really hard and set MIT as my goal. I eventually got 9As and 1B in my SPM.

      Skytrex Adventure provides the first of its kind in Malaysia, a tree to tree ‘sky-trekking’ experience where you can fly, swing, glide and dangle on the various aerial obstacles suspended above the lush tropical Malaysian Rainforest. Take up the challenge and pump up the adrenaline on our many circuits at the various parks in which we are located and experience the best and most exciting forest adventure in Malaysia.
