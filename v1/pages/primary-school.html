    My parents always tell me that I was an extremely obedient child. I was very easy to be taken care of because wherever I go, I would just sleep. But I grew up to be an extremely rebelious teenager though..

    Stage Fright

    I used to have stage fright. Still, I always participate in my primary school's story telling competitions. I am not sure why. Maybe because I have a pair of . My parents did not force me, that's for sure.

    Anyway, during one of the competitions when I was in Standard 5 or 6, I was selected as one of the winners. I knew this because I was at the backstage with one of my friends. I was so happy and I returned to my seat knowing that I "am" going to win despite my usual poor performance. Sadly, during the announcement of winnders, I was not the winner of third place. In fact, my friend who was at the backstage, told me that after I left, the teachers discussed and said my performance was not up to par and they "did the right thing".

    Last Nightmare

    I had my last nightmare at the age of about 8 or 9 years old. I used to have a lot of nightmares. I got sick of it and every time I woke up, I told myself that it was all fake. No monsters exist!

    So one day, I was having a nightmare again. I still remember the dream very clearly. I was running away from a bunch of monsters and suddenly, I "woke up". I knew it was all fake and I stopped running. I basically controlled my dream from that point onwards. No joke. I wanted the monsters to start running away from me and they did. I wanted a laser gun to shoot them and I did.

    Business

    Who would have guessed I was a good businessman when I was a kid? I used to love drawing. I loved it so much I started selling my drawings to my schoolmates. I drew Spiderman, PowerPuff Girls, Batman, Masked Riders, etc. in a book which I used as a catalogue. All the drawings are labelled with price ranging from 10 cents to 1 Ringgit. My schoolmates would choose the pictures that they want and pay me after I completed the drawing. I was earning good money man!

    After some time, I started drawing comics. I first began with stickman drawings and eventually started drawing real comics but the layout was very bad.
