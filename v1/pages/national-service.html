    Boring Stuffs

    Honestly, except meeting new friends and some of the fun activities, most of the national service program was just boring. Everyday we were required to go to class. We had to sit down on the floor and listened to the trainers talking about patriotisme and drew about how's our day yesterday using crayons, etc.

    Sparring

    Some of the trainees started organizing their own sparring contest. The main reason was to allow two trainees who were dissatisfied with each other fight fair and square without interuption.The rules were:

    • Do not attack head. This is so that injuries were not visible to the trainers.
    • Do not use weapon. For obvious reasons.
    • No one speaks about it. For obvious reasons too.
    • No one interupts until the fight is over.
    • If there is a trainer nearby, no matter how intense the fight is, stop immediately.

    Wang LeeHom

    For who knows what reason, Wang LeeHom's Still in Love With You mandarin song became a big hit in the camp. Everyone was crazy with this song man! I was not one of them though. This song was such a big hit even the non-Chinese started singing it every day!!! I am not kidding!
